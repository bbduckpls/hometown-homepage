Visit Vista

Enjoy a rich blend of tradition and agriculture, 7 miles off the Pacific Ocean

Vista, California

Population: 97,766

Location: Northern San Diego County

Climate: Mediterranean climate with mild, wet winters and hot, dry summers.

History: Vista, California, was originally settled by Spanish missionaries in the late 18th century, becoming an agricultural hub known for its citrus and avocado groves. It officially became a city in 1963 and has since grown into a vibrant community with a rich blend of cultural and historical influences.
